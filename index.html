Why Commercial Growers Choose PP Crates

Vented for Freshness

Integrated ventilation allows airflow and ethylene release — extends shelf life during cold chain transport.

100+ Reuses

Each PP crate replaces a full season's worth of cardboard boxes. Per-use cost drops dramatically after the first harvest.

Waterproof

Performs in irrigation, rain, and refrigerated environments where cardboard fails. No collapse, no contamination.

Recycled & Recyclable

Made from recycled PP and fully recyclable at end of life. Meets sustainability requirements for major retailers and food service buyers.

Custom Sizing

Standard and custom dimensions for specific vegetables, palletizing patterns, and retailer specifications.

Food Grade

FDA-compliant PP available for direct contact with fresh produce, roots, and leafy vegetables.
